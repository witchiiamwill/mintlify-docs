Hunter Character Creation:\
\
Attributes: 6/4/3\
Start with 1 dot in each.\
We go with the lower Attribute spend since we allow the bumped Ability stats.\
\
Abilities: 13/9/5\
No more than 3 before FBP.\
4 dots allowed with EXP with really good back story.\
No 5 dots at char gen. Must be earned in roleplay.\
\
Backgrounds: 5\
Armory: allowed weapons within reason. Need Admin approval.\
Resources: 50k per dot in all splats. If exceeding 3, it really needs good explanation why. We don't want people dot stacking for free money.\
Haven/Safehouse: We will not be giving any property so this is moot.\
Backgrounds may be purchased after char gen for current level x 3\
\
Virtues: 7 \
Start with 1 dot in each.\
\
Numina: Must have REALLY good details as to why they would have this gift essentially bestowed by god.\
\
Merits and Flaws:\
Allowed to take appropriate VTM V20, Hunters Hunted II, and any Merit or Flaw listed on the Mortal Merit and Flaw page here: [https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1ZrnBEkJHQHE3ISBllqh8b1YCDL9kpC6GiZRysZy2UIE/edit?pli=1](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1ZrnBEkJHQHE3ISBllqh8b1YCDL9kpC6GiZRysZy2UIE/edit?pli=1)\
Please make sure you are staying up to date on banned merits and flaws! \
\
We allow players to take up to 7 points in merits and up to 7 points max in flaws. RAW allows players to buy as many merits as they can afford but we cap those for balancing reasons.\
\
Flaws may be purchased off with appropriate documented roleplay at flaw level x 3.\
\
Freebie Points to Spend: 21\
\
EXP awarded should be ROUGHLY characters age x 1. You can give or take as needed based on back story. If they give a good back story and such you can give more than their age. If it's lacking, drop it down. Plus or Minus 5 at max please. \
\
Hunters should have well thought out back stories and have a clear understanding that a Hunter comes with a Perma clause. This means that if a hunter is found out, they do not need a ticket to be perma killed by another player.
