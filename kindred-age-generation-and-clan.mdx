---
title: "Kindred Age, Generation, and Clan"
---

Kindred measure power with age, generation, and clan often with each other. Knowledge is power after all in the world of darkness. One of the biggest mistake new players make is giving away these 3 core components of their characters. 

Age - Age is such an important factor with kindred as it helps define where you sit in a hierarchy of experience. Lived life is equally as important as generation and clan. Experience is knowledge and knowledge is power. The older you are, the less likely it is a kindred will try any funny business.

Generation - Generation defines your kindred max potential as well as how far they are from Caine. A fledgling that reveals they are 9th gen to a 70 year old neonate who is 11th gen is prime pickings for diablerie. It's only frowned upon if you get caught. \
\
Clan - Some kindred are much more open with their clan than others. A Brujah may have no trouble boasting about their clan but others like Salubri, may not be so keen to share what clan they hail from. Each clan has stigmas, known strength and weaknesses (within reason), and rough concepts of idea. A Malkavian may not want to take a head on fight with a Brujah in close range but they can pivot to utilize other tactics to take out these power houses if they have that information.

Its important to know your clans general stigma, how others view them, and what information you share. In the life of the damned, money means little and knowledge is the richest a kindred can be. Safe guard this information and be careful who you share it with! Combine all 3 together for any kindred, and you have a very good idea of potential power output and how you may need to take them out. Kindred are manipulative, cunning, and quick to plunge a knife in someones back the first chance hey get. Don't make the same mistake others do in the shadows where the kindred hide.
