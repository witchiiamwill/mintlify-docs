## RAVNOS WL QUESTIONNAIRE\
\
### 1) GENERAL QUESTIONS\
- Why do you want to play a Ravnos?\
- What draws you to them beyond “trickster” or “chaotic” archetypes? What is the Path of Paradox? What is the Week of Nightmares ?\
\
### 2) VALUES AND MINDSET\
\*Ravnos often embody survival, illusion, and displacement.\*\
 - What core theme does your character represent, and how does it shape their behavior?\
\
\*After the Week of Nightmares, the clan is fragmented and decentralized.\*\
- How does your character relate to other Ravnos?\
- What impact did the Week of Nightmare have on your character and how did they live through it if they did?\
\
\*Ravnos are driven by a personal compulsion tied to vice or deception.\*\
- What is your character’s compulsion, and how do they manage it in nightly unlife?\
- Describe a time your character’s compulsion caused harm or risk.\
- Did they accept responsibility, deny it, or twist the narrative?\
- Does your character value truth, or the story people believe?\
- Explain with an example of how they manipulate (or refuse to manipulate) perception.\
\
### 3) PICK AND WRITE _TWO_ OF THE THREE FOLLOWING IC SCENES\
\*You use your abilities or lie to solve a problem, but it risks breaking the Masquerade. Another Kindred notices and confronts you privately.\*\
What do you say or do at that moment?\
\
\*You meet another Ravnos who fully embraces destructive impulses, leaving chaos behind. They insist this is the clan’s true nature.\*\
How does your character react, and what does this reveal about their identity?\
\
\*You’ve built a rare, genuine bond with someone (Kindred or mortal).\
 Your compulsion pushes you to betray them in a way that would benefit you significantly.\*\
Write a short response (dialogue or internal monologue):\
 Do you give in, resist, or find another path—and how do you justify it?
