## TREMERE WL QUESTIONNAIRE\
\
### 1) GENERAL QUESTIONS\
- Why do you want to play a Tremere?\
- What interests you beyond “blood magic” or raw power?\
\
\*The Tremere were not always Kindred.\*\
- Explain how the Tremere Clan was created.\
- What is the Pyramid, and why is it essential to the Tremere?\
- What purpose does it serve beyond simple hierarchy? How does it align with the Sects?\
- How are Tremere “linked” together within the Pyramid? How does this limit or shape individual freedom?\
- Name and describe at least three roles or ranks within the Pyramid. How do they interact in practice?\
- What is a Chantry, and what role does it play in Tremere society? How does your character fit into one?\
\
### 2) VALUES AND MINDSET\
- Was your character a ghoul before being Embraced? If so, for how long?\
- What made them worthy, and what did they prove to deserve the Embrace and their access to Thaumaturgy?\
- Did they have any interest in the Occult before their Embrace ?\
- How does your character balance loyalty to the Pyramid with personal ambition?\
Do they conform, scheme, or try to do both?\
\
\*Thaumaturgy is controlled knowledge.\*\
- How does your character approach learning blood magic?\
- How do they react to restrictions on forbidden or higher-level knowledge?\
\
### 3) PICK AND WRITE _TWO_ OF THE THREE FOLLOWING IC SCENES\
\*Your Regent gives you an order that conflicts with your personal goals.\
Refusal risks punishment and loss of status.\*\
- What do you do, and how do you justify it?\
\
\*You uncover Thaumaturgical knowledge you are not authorized to possess.\*\
\*Reporting it earns favor.\*\
\*Keeping it gives you power.\*\
- What choice do you make, and why?\
\
\*You are mystically and socially bound to the Pyramid and ordered to act against someone you trust.\*\
\*Defiance is not just dangerous—it may feel impossible due to conditioning and blood bonds.\*\
- Write a short response (dialogue or internal monologue):\
- How do you respond, and what does it reveal about your place in the Pyramid?
