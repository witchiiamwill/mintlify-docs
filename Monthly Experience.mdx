<u>Monthly Experience:</u>\
\
Monthly experience is what will drive our characters to want to advance their characters and buy new things on their sheet.\
\
We should always be aiming to help players grow and evolve in roleplay.\
\
Max EXP each month is 12 points for any splay. Players can ear up to 2 additional experience from participating in ST Plot lines. This can be rewarded in their ticket by an ST. Please make sure you let players know to mention their involvement in your plot lines so they can get credit! \
\
\
**<u>Monthly EXP Questions:</u>**

- What is your Characters name?
- What is their Race, Age, and any tribe/clan affiliations?
- What has your character done or experienced this month?
- What ways do you feel your character has grown this month?
- From 0 to 12, how much EXP do you think your character has earned this month? Why?
- Have you participated in any Story Teller run lines? If so, what were they?
- Depending on awarded experience, how would you like to spend your experience this month? Please list out what you wish to take, the dot level, and cost. If you need help, a Story Teller can assist you with this.
- Please drop the old and new sheet with your proposed changes.
