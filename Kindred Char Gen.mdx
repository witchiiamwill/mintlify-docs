Kindred Character Creation:\
Age ranges:\
Fledgling - Still in accounting. Can range in age. Must come with Sire.\
Neonate - 10 to 100 years \+ Max 10th Gen\
Ancilla - 100 to 250 years \+ Max 9th Gen\
Elder - 300 years \+ Max 8th Gen\
\
Attributes: 7/5/3\
Start with 1 dot in each.\
\
Abilities: 13/9/5\
No more than 3 before FBP.\
4 dots allowed with EXP with really good back story.\
5 dots at char gen must have REALLY good explanation. Younger kindred really shouldn't have 5 dots unless earned in roleplay.\
\
Backgrounds: 5\
Armory: allowed weapons within reason. Need Admin approval.\
Resources: 50k per dot in all splats. If exceeding 3, it really needs good explanation why. We don't want people dot stacking for free money.\
Haven/Safehouse: We will not be giving any property so this is moot.\
Backgrounds may be purchased after char gen for current level x 3\
\
Virtues: 7 \
Start with 1 dot in each.\
\
Humanity/paths: \
A kindred may come in on a path but it needs to be well justified in story. If ever in doubt, tag the head Kindred ST or an Admin.\
\
Disciplines: \
- Young kindred should not discipline stack. Discipline stacking is where they dump everything into one discipline (like a Gangrel only taking Prtean 4) and nothing in other disciplines. \
- Out of clan disciplines at Char Gen need to be well explained in back story.\
- Learning Out of clan disciplines need to have at least 1 sip of the kindreds blood in roleplay effectively 1 step blood bonding them to that kindred. \
-They must also have sufficient roleplay to back learning the dot. This can be documented by the player and submitted for ST approval.\
\
Merits and Flaws:\
- Allowed to take appropriate VTM V20 Merits or Flaws and from the Kindred page here: [https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1ZrnBEkJHQHE3ISBllqh8b1YCDL9kpC6GiZRysZy2UIE/edit?pli=1](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1ZrnBEkJHQHE3ISBllqh8b1YCDL9kpC6GiZRysZy2UIE/edit?pli=1)\
Please make sure you are staying up to date on banned merits and flaws! \
- We allow players to take up to 7 points in merits and up to 7 points max in flaws. RAW allows players to buy as many merits as they can afford but we cap those for balancing reasons.\
- Flaws may be purchased off with appropriate documented roleplay at flaw level x 3.\
\
Freebie Points to Spend: 15\
\
Kindred EXP: EXP awarded should be ROUGHLY characters age x 0.6 for Kindred. You can give or take as needed based on back story. If they give a good back story and such you can give more than their age. If it's lacking, drop it down. Plus or Minus 5 at max please. Max experience is capped at 300 for elders.\
\
Ghoul EXP: EXP awarded should be ROUGHLY characters age x 1 for mortal years and ghoul years x 0.5. You can give or take as needed based on back story. If they give a good back story and such you can give more than their age. If it's lacking, drop it down. Plus or Minus 5 at max please. The ghoul must have a player character domitor who is ALREADY approved. \

Revenants and Ghouls:\
Ghouls:\
- Must come with a domitor.\
- Follow Mortal Creation \+ Give 1 dot in Potence in disciplines and 1 in clan.\
- Should not be learning out of clan disciplines.\
- If switching domitors, they retain their knowledge of the discipline as well as access to use it. \
- If switching domitors, they do not get a new discipline dot from that new domitor. The free in clan dot is a one time per the life of the character to avoid stat maxing.\
