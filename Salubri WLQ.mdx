```text
## SALUBRI WL QUESTIONNAIRE
1) GENERAL QUESTIONS
- Why do you want to play a Salubri?
- What draws you to them beyond “they heal” or “they’re rare”?
- Why are Salubri hunted, and by whom?
- How much does your character know about this—and how has it shaped their behavior?
- How are Salubri generally perceived by other Kindred in modern nights?
- How does your character handle this suspicion or hostility?
- Salubri are divided into major paths.
- Is your character a Healer or a Warrior Salubri?
- Why did you choose this path?
- How does it affect their worldview, methods, and relationship to violence?

2) VALUES AND MINDSET
- What core belief or purpose drives your character?
 - Redemption, Golconda, protection, judgment, or something else?

*Salubri powers are difficult to hide.*
- How do your character’s abilities manifest (visually, emotionally, spiritually)?
- How do they manage the risk of being discovered?
- What concrete steps does your character take to avoid being hunted?
- Do they isolate, blend in, or rely on others?

3) PICK AND WRITE TWO OF THE THREE FOLLOWING IC SCENES
*Another Kindred begins to suspect your true nature.*
*They’ve noticed something “off” about you.*
- What do you do or say to deflect or survive this situation?

*Someone is suffering, and you can help—but doing so risks exposing you.*
- Do you act or hold back?
- Explain your decision and how your character justifies it.

*You discover another Salubri is about to be hunted.*
*Intervening risks your unlife.*
*Doing nothing ensures their destruction.*
- Write a short response (dialogue or internal monologue):
 What choice do you make, and why?
```
