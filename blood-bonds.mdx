---
title: "Blood Bonds"
---

Blood Bonds are a potent element of Vampire: The Masquerade. Blood Bonds tie a mortal or kindred to a domitor in ways many won't understand. Below you will find a quick reference guild to let players know the effects of the bond.\
\
It is important to note that in Vampire: The Masquerade 20th Edition, a person must drink the blood straight from the source unless it is magically preserved with Thaumaturgy. Kindred blood looses its potence and effectiveness outside of the body. If tested, it would seem to be normal human blood. That's because the blood in a kindred is the blood they have stolen from others.\

At 1 step, the one who is being bonded will have intense emotions towards the domitor. They may unintentional find themself drawn to places that the kindred is. The intense emotions can be good or bad. It is still relatively easy to disobey an order.\
\
At 2 steps, emotions turn to only positive ones. This can be a protective or even loving draw. The bond makes it generally hard to disobey an order but not impossible at this point. The one being bonded will often seek out the domitor and wish to be closer with them.\
\
At 3 steps, the bond is complete. No one else can fully bond that character until the bond is severed or dissipates on it's own. At 3 steps, resisting a command is temporary at best no matter how outlandish the command is. If your domitor orders you to kill your best friend, you can spend a will power to temporarily ignore the command. Eventually, you will have to comply with the order. Your domitor means more to you than life itself at this point. You are hopelessly devoted to them.

Bonding someone, be it a mortal or kindred, is a huge deal. Mortals lose all sense of agency and become a thrall to the kindred. Kindred who are bonded by other kindred aren't much different. Being bonded to another kindred is often taboo, frowned upon, or even used as a punishment.

When a mortal is bonded, they are called a ghoul.

When a kindred is bonded, they are just blood bound.

When two kindred bond each other, this is called a Blood Wedding. It is very taboo and often end poorly as the kindred often have high volatile emotions for each other. They often do anything for the other and it shows. Other kindred may frown upon this because you essentially don't have agency to act on your own with a clear head. If you are a kindred who practices this, its best to keep it very secret.
