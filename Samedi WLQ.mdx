```text
## SAMEDI WL QUESTIONNAIRE

### 1) GENERAL QUESTIONS
- Why do you want to play a Samedi?
- What interests you beyond appearance or shock value?

*Samedi embody rot and the boundary between life and death.*
- What does “being dead while still acting” mean to your character?
- How does this shape their personality?

*Samedi have unclear and debated origins in Kindred society.* 
- Does this matter to them?
- What does your character believe about where their bloodline comes from? 
- What do they know about Baron Samedi?

### 2) VALUES AND MINDSET
*Samedi are visibly decayed and cannot easily pass as human.*
- How does your character deal with their appearance on a nightly basis?
Socially, practically, and psychologically.
- How does your character define themselves beyond being a Samedi?
- What keeps them going night after night?
- How are Samedi generally treated by other Kindred?
- How does your character navigate this treatment?

*Samedi are closely tied to death and often interact with it directly.*
- How does your character view death?
- How does your character function in society despite their condition? - - How do they see the Sects ?
- Does your character still relate to humanity or have they moved beyond it?
- Explain where they stand emotionally.

### 3) PICK AND WRITE TWO OF THE THREE FOLLOWING IC SCENES
*You must interact with mortals despite your decayed appearance. Avoiding them is not an option.*
- How do you handle this situation?

*You are in a gathering of other Kindred who clearly view you with disgust or distrust. You need something from them.*
- How do you approach the situation?

*You realize your condition is worsening physically and mentally. You are becoming more corpse than person.*
- Write a short response (dialogue or internal monologue): Do you resist, accept, or redefine yourself and how do you justify it?

```
