## CAITIFF WL QUESTIONNAIRE\
### 1) GENERAL QUESTIONS\
- Why do you want to play a Caitiff?\
- What interests you beyond “undisciplined vampire” or “outcast”?\
\
### 2) VALUES AND MINDSET\
\*Caitiff lack a defined clan identity.\*\
 - How does your character interpret being clanless? How did they become Caitiff?\
- How does you character navigate among the different Sects? Avoid, comply, allly, something else?\
- How did your character develop their Disciplines without clan teaching?\
 - What do they struggle with due to this lack of guidance?\
- How are Caitiff treated by other Kindred, and how does your character respond?\
\
### 3) PICK AND WRITE TWO OF THE THREE FOLLOWING IC SCENES\
\
\*At Elysium, another Kindred publicly calls you “clanless trash.”\*\
 - What do you do or say?\
\
\*You are offered protection or inclusion by an Anarch baron, but only if you accept being treated as inferior.\*\
- Do you accept, refuse, or manipulate the situation? Why?\
\
\*You discover evidence that you might have a clan, but it is unclear or contradictory.\*\
 - Write a short response (dialogue or internal monologue):\
 - Do you pursue the truth, reject it, or redefine who you are?
