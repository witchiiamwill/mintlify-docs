## THIN BLOOD WL QUESTIONNAIRE\
\
### 1) GENERAL QUESTIONS\
- Why do you want to play a Thin-Blood?\
- What makes a Thin-Blood different from other Kindred?\
. How does your character define themselves being in-between Kindred and mortal?\
\
### 2) VALUES AND MINDSET\
\*Thin-Bloods often lack the raw power of older Kindred.\*\
- How does your character compensate for their relative weakness?\
- How are Thin-Bloods generally viewed by other Kindred?\
- How does your character respond to this treatment?\
- How connected is your character to humanity?\
- Do they still think of themselves as human?\
\
\*Thin-Bloods can manifest unusual or unstable traits.\*\
 - What makes your character’s condition unusual or difficult to manage?\
- How do they handle it?\
- Many Thin-Bloods struggle with purpose.\
- What keeps your character going night after night?\
\
\*Thin-Bloods are often associated with Gehenna fears and apocalyptic beliefs.\*\
- Does your character know or care about these prophecies? - How does this affect them?\
\
### 3) PICK AND WRITE TWO OF THE THREE FOLLOWING IC SCENES\
\*At Elysium, someone openly dismisses you as “barely Kindred.” Others are watching.\* \
- How do you react?\
\
\*A powerful Kindred offers protection and status, but only if you become their obedient tool.\* \
- Do you accept, refuse, or exploit the offer? Why?\
\
\*You are offered a dangerous way to become a “true” vampire, at great personal cost. It may destroy what makes you you.\*\
- Write a short response (dialogue or internal monologue): \
- Do you take it, reject it, or hesitate, and why?
