```text
## TZIMISCE WL QUESTIONNAIRE

### 1) GENERAL QUESTIONS
- Why do you want to play a Tzimisce?
- What interests you beyond “body horror” or Vicissitude?

### 2) VALUES AND MINDSET
*Tzimisce are defined by control and ownership.*
 - What does “domain” mean to your character?
 Is it land, people, identity, or something else?

*Tzimisce must rest in native soil or a deeply tied resting place.*
- What is your character’s relationship to their soil/haven?
- Where does it come from?
- What happens if they are separated from it?
- What role does your character take on?
Territorial lord?
Scholar of flesh?
War leader?
Recluse experimenter?
- How does this role shape their nightly behavior?

*Tzimisce rarely remain on Humanity.*
- Which Path does your character follow?
Path of Metamorphosis?
Humanity?
Another Path?
- Why this choice, and what does it say about their long-term goal?

### 3) PICK AND WRITE TWO OF THE THREE FOLLOWING IC SCENES
*Tzimisce are historically tied to the Sabbat—but not all think the same.*
- How does your character view the Sabbat?
Loyal member?
Useful tool?
Failed ideology?
Something to exploit or avoid?
- Explain their stance and why.

*How does your character view Vicissitude and transformation?*
- Self-perfection, transcendence, control over others, or something else?

*Another Kindred enters your territory and disrespects your authority.*
- How do you respond, and what does this reveal about your sense of ownership?

*The Sabbat (or a powerful faction within it) demands your participation in a cause that does not align with your personal goals.*
- Do you comply, manipulate the situation, or resist? Why?

*Your pursuit of transformation is stripping away what remains of your original self.*
You are close to achieving something greater—but less human.
- Write a short response (dialogue or internal monologue):
Do you embrace this evolution, redefine it, or resist it—and why?
```
