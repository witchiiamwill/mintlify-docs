## LASOMBRA WL QUESTIONNAIRE\
\
### 1) GENERAL QUESTIONS\
- Why do you want to play a Lasombra?\
- What draws you to them specifically over other clans?\
\
### 2) VALUES AND MINDSET\
\*Lasombra reject weakness and value control.\*\
- What does “strength” mean to your character, and how do they measure it in others?\
\
\*Lasombra often destroy or betray to rise.\*\
- Describe a moment when your character chose ambition over loyalty.\
- What did it cost them?\
\
- How does your character justify manipulation, domination, or cruelty? - Where (if anywhere) do they draw the line?\
\
\*Lasombra don’t just win, they control the chessboard.\* \
- Describe how your character handles someone they cannot intimidate or dominate.\
\
\*Obtenebration reflects darkness and control.\* \
- What do the Shadows symbolize to your character personally?\
\
- Would your character rather be feared or respected? Why? \
- Give an example of how they enforce that.\
\
### 3) PICK AND WRITE TWO OF THE THREE FOLLOWING IC SCENES\
\
Write a small IC scene: \
\*'You ordered a ghoul to handle a problem. They failed, and now it threatens your status.'\*\
\*Others are watching.\*\
\*The ghoul is loyal but incompetent.\*\
- What do you do in the moment and what happens after?\
\
Write a small IC scene: \
\*'You have no choice and/or are ordered to betray someone who trusts you deeply because it will advance your standing.'\*\
\*Refusal means losing status or worse.\*\
\
Write a small IC scene: \
\*'You’re at Elysium. A rival publicly insults your competence.'\*\
\*You cannot attack them directly.\*\
- What do you do, say, or set in motion?
