## REVENANT WL QUESTIONNAIRE\
\
1) GENERAL QUESTIONS\
- Why do you want to play a Revenant?\
- What interests you beyond “better ghoul” or “vampire-lite”?\
- What makes a Revenant different from a normal ghoul?\
\
2) VALUES AND MINDSET\
- Revenants are born into servitude and shaped from birth.\
 - What family does your character come from, and how were they raised to view vampires?\
\
\*Duty, fear, reverence, ambition, or something else?\*\
- What is your character’s role within their family or domitor’s domain?\
\
\*Servant, overseer, enforcer, administrator, or something more specialized?\*\
\*Revenants often serve powerful Kindred.\*\
- How does your character view their domitor?\
- To what extent does your character think for themselves?\
\
\*Are they loyal by belief, fear, conditioning - or do they question their place?\*\
\*Revenants are not fully human in mindset.\*\
- How does your character view ordinary mortals?\
Equals, tools, inferiors, or something else?\
\
3) PICK AND WRITE _TWO_ OF THE THREE FOLLOWING IC SCENES\
\*Your family orders you to carry out a task that will harm innocent people. Refusal would bring consequences not just to you, but to your relatives.\*\
What do you do, and how do you justify it?\
\
\*You discover your domitor has been lying to you about something fundamental (your purpose, your family’s role, or your future).\*\
- Do you confront them, suppress your doubts, or act differently going forward? Why?\
\
\*You are offered the chance to be Embraced - or to break free entirely from your role. Accepting means losing what you are. Refusing means remaining bound.\*\
- Write a short response (dialogue or internal monologue):\
\*What do you choose, and how do you understand your own identity at that moment?\*
