\
When it comes to groups, we need to be cognizant of the following:\\

- Packs, gangs, and coteries are limited to a max 12 members. Kinfolk are counted in packs just like ghouls are counted for coteries.\
- Baronry is a word for a kindred's territory and those who rule it. A Baron runs the territory with their coterie. The Reeve should also be in the Baron's coterie. Other coteries may form and live in the baronry but are expected to follow the territory rules or race consequences from the Baron.\
- Gangs can be mixed of any type of people. Players can be in a gang and a pack or coterie but need to be mindful of double dipping. If we are concerned about super groups forming, we can revisit this as needed.
